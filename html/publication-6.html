Orizon: A modular urban garden

Orizon is a collection of modular planters designed for modern urban gardening. The project focused on design-for-manufacturing using industrial rotational molding. The final concept consists of hollow, interlocking components that allow everyday users to configure and expand their vertical urban garden through integrated horizontal or vertical coupling systems.

This project sparked my passion for design-for-manufacturing, though it also taught me the vital importance of time management after a late start forced an intense final sprint. Ultimately, pushing through those last two weeks to deliver a high-quality result is something I am very proud of.
