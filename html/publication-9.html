Portable Speakers

19

CAD Solid modeling

The objective of this project was to reverse-engineer a JBL Go speaker with absolute precision, measuring and modeling every component 1-to-1. Additionally, the project required leveraging advanced CAD features; using design tables and configurations within a single file, I successfully created three alternative design variants of the product.

This project significantly advanced my solid modeling skills, with the biggest challenge being the management of four distinct product variations through configurations within a single file.
