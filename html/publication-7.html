Speaker Disassembly & Optimization

The objective of this structural design assignment was to reverse-engineer a portable speaker. The project involved complete product disassembly, extensive prototyping, precise 3D CAD reconstruction, and optimizing the internal components to streamline the overall assembly process while ensuring efficient production and manufacturability.

Working in a team of three, my primary role was leading the 3D development—ideating sub-solutions and modeling the final integrated CAD assembly.
