Vacuum Cleaner

21

CAD Surface modeling

This CAD project served as the ultimate test for my surfacing skills. The goal was to analyze a real-world modern vacuum cleaner and replicate its complex parts as realistically as possible in CAD. It was a great opportunity to master complex, free-form shapes that are difficult to achieve with standard solid modeling. Throughout the process, special attention was paid to aesthetic quality, high digital precision, and seamless surface continuity.

Achieving advanced curvature continuity across the complex transitions was a real struggle, but mastering it was incredibly rewarding.
