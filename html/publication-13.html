Enzo Mari Inspired: Reclaimed Wood Workbench

This personal project was inspired by a book featuring the furniture designs of Enzo Mari. Drawing inspiration from one of his chairs, I designed and built a custom workbench tailored to my hobby of vegetable gardening. The workbench is constructed entirely from 100% reclaimed pallet and waste wood, which I stripped of nails and varnished.
