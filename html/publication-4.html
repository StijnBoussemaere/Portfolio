Pure Paper Lighting

The objective of this design assignment was to develop a functional ambient lamp designed to create atmosphere during the evening hours, using sustainable Favini paper as the sole structural medium. The project focused on exploring material constraints and light diffusion, engineering a self-supporting diffuser exclusively through paper connections without the use of glue, staples, or tape.

As my first design assignment, this project was a challenging introduction to creating a coherent final product. Through extensive trial and error, it taught me that perseverance and continuous prototyping will always lead to a successful solution.
