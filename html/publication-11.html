CO-BEE: A Smart Bee-Hotel

My bachelor thesis focused on the development of a smart bee hotel designed to monitor the nesting activity of solitary bees. The project involved the integration of sensing technology, embedded electronics, solar power, enclosure design, and manufacturability considerations. The resulting product serves as an ecological monitoring tool that contributes to biodiversity research and citizen science initiatives.

Through this project, I discovered a deep passion for electronics and software development. I am incredibly proud of the execution and personally feel that my bachelor thesis could not have gone much better.
